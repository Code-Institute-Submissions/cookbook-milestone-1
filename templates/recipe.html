Pumpkin curry with chickpeas

Prep time: 20 minutes

Cooking time: none

Serves: 4

Skill level: Easy

This delicious breaded fish with sweet potato fries is the perfect mid-week treat.

Ingredients:

4 x 140g portions traditionally smoked haddock

knob of butter

1 lemon, quartered

Pack of 4 Bubble & Squeak Cakes (or potato rösti)

4 large eggs

4 handfuls spinach, washed

black pepper

1 pack of ready-made hollandaise sauce (optional)

Pinch dried Dulse seaweed flakes (optional)

Method:

  1. Preheat the oven to 180C/fan 160C/gas
  2. Place the haddock, butter and 2 lemon quarters on a piece of. foil. Wrap carefully in the foil and place on a baking sheet with the Bubble & Squeak Cakes.
  3. Bake in the oven for 15 minutes, turning the cakes halfway through the cooking time.
  4. A few minutes before the fish is ready, bring a pan of water to a simmer. Poach the eggs for a few minutes, until cooked to your liking.Drain the eggs carefully on kitchen paper.
  5. Place the spinach in a colander and pour over boiling water until it’s wilted. Drain well.
  6. Remove the tray from the oven. Put the bubble and squeak cakes on 4 plates and top with the spinach. Carefully unwrap the fish and place a piece on top of each of the bubble and squeak cakes and the spinach, drizzling over the buttery juices.
  7. Top with the eggs, a sprinkling of seaweed flakes (if using), and some black pepper. Serve with the hollandaise sauce (if using) and the remaining lemon.
